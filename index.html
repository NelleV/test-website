Welcome!

The CompBio@TrEE is the computational biology group of the TrEE team (TIMC) in Grenoble. Our research is dedicated to the development of methods and tools to analyse, interpret, and decipher biological data and process, with a focus on evolution of prokaryotes

We are composed of specialists in comparative genomics, machine learning, and biophysics
