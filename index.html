Welcome!

The CompBio@TrEE is the computational biology group of the TrEE team (TIMC) in Grenoble. Our research is dedicated to the development of methods and tools to analyse, interpret, and decipher biological data and process, with a focus on the evolution of prokaryotes.

We are composed of experts in comparative genomics, machine learning, and biophysics.

Highlights

  • The 3D structure of genomes
  • Evolution of biosynthetic pathways
  • Evolution of complex cellular machineries
  • Development of methods to annotate genomes
  • Development of methods to analyse various omics data
  • and more...
